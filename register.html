Apply for Junior Executive


Waterloo DECA is looking for smart, energetic and creative junior executives to help manage chapter operations and organize our annual Intrapreneurship Conference held in March. Junior executives will be paired with a senior mentor in the fall, and will control a specific function (logistics, graphics/video, marketing, finance, training, sponsorship, and speakers) during the winter semester. Advance your professional career with Waterloo and DECA U's premier club!

Want to join the Team? Submit your resume and written application to applications@uwdeca.com before midnight on Friday, September 28th. The written application can be found below. Successful candidates will be contacted and invited to interview.

Questions about the club or internship positions can be directed to admin@uwdeca.com.

What We Offer

Leadership

There are many opportunities in DECA to be part of a team and influence others, whether it is through an executive role or being a leader in competition. DECA offers an environment where these skills can be developed and practiced.


Experiential Learning

By being part of DECA you will be surrounded by like-minded individuals that have a passion for business and competition. Through multiple events in the school year, skills will be developed through hands-on experiences. The learnings from DECA will be used in business simulations, networking with professionals, and competitions.


Networking

Several of the conferences throughout the year offer networking sessions packed with industry professionals. Leveraging these sessions can create lasting connections and even a chance at internships.


Career and Professional Development

Ranging from dining etiquette to networking your way to a job, DECA has events covering many aspects of the professional corporate world - often held by the professionals themselves.


Recognition and Awards

All UW DECA programmes recognize students and advisors for their leadership, commitment to education, individual accomplishment and teamwork.
