University of Waterloo DECA

Waterloo DECA is a chapter in an international association of students who share a mutual passion for business and participating in case competitions. The organization maintains a strong focus in business-related areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, management, and entrepreneurship.


Details

Waterloo DECA
Since 2000, Waterloo DECA has been operating under the Federation of Students (FEDS) at the University of Waterloo, attracting students of various educational disciplines across the six faculties. The organization aims to improve leadership, communication, and critical thinking skills through professional development conferences and enriching training programs.

DECA U
DECA U Ontario is the umbrella organization of DECA chapters in over 20 Ontario universities and colleges, including Waterloo DECA. The annual DECA U Provincial Competition is hosted at the Sheraton Hotel in downtown Toronto every January.

DECA
DECA Inc. is the international parent association and was founded in 1946. It is now operating in 200 universities and colleges across Canada, the United States, Puerto Rico, and Guam. DECA Inc. also hosts the annual International Career Development Conference (ICDC) in the United States.

FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Why should I join DECA?
Joining DECA is a decision that has the potential to change your career and promote professional development.

Here are just 3 simple reasons to join:
Advance your professional career and network.
Enrich your profile of business and leadership experience.
Demonstrate your business excellence at case competitions.
What do members do as a part of UW Deca?
While there are no regular meetings, members attend a number of professional development programs and competitive events throughout the year, including:

Waterloo In-House case competition
Training sessions
DECA U Provincials / ICDC
Invitational conferences hosted by other university DECA chapters
Annual Waterloo DECA Conference

Check out our calendar on the Events page for more details!
How do I find out about upcoming events?
The best way to find out about our events is to become a member, like, or follow us!
How much are membership fees?
$10 for Members that do not wish to participate in DECA U events such as Provincials and ICDC.
$30 for Members that do wish to participate in DECA U events.
