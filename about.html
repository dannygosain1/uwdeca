What is DECA?
DECA is an international organization of students who share a mutual passion for business and participating in intense case competitions. The organization aims to improve leadership, communication, and critical thinking skills through professional development and enrichment programs.
Why should I join?
Joining DECA is a decision that has the potential to change your career and professional development. Here are just 3 simple reasons to join:

  1. Advance your professional career and network.
  2. Enrich your profile of business and leadership experience.
  3. Demonstrate your business excellence at case competitions.
How much are membership fees?
$10 for Members that do not wish to participate in DECA U events (DLP, Provincials, ICDC)
$30 for Members that wish to participate in DECA U events
How do I find out about events?
The best way to find out about our events is to become a member, like, or follow us!
What/When is Provincials?
Provincials is an event hosted by DECA U, inviting all DECA U Ontario chapters to the Sheraton Hotel in Downtown Toronto for DECA competition, workshops, and socials. It takes place on January 18-19, 2013.
What is the Waterloo DECA Conference?
The Waterloo DECA Conference is an annual 2-day conference at the University of Waterloo, themed around the concept of "Intrapreneurship" in business. While our conference retains the much-loved, traditional DECA competition, we also offer an elimination-style consulting pitch competition and industry speakers/workshops. Our conference network includes large firms such as PwC and KPMG, as well as successful start-ups that represent the innovative spirit Waterloo is famous for.
I still have questions!
